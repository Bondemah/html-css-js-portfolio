Who We Are

Flare is a branding agency based in somewhere. We design thoughtful digital experiences and beautiful brand aesthetics.

The Seventh Creative Agency was established with the purpose of supporting young people in developing their creative abilities while showcasing the richness of Ghanaian and African creativity to the world. We are a youth-led, Christ-centered creative agency dedicated to empowering the next generation of creatives to make meaningful contributions both locally and globally.

Our vision is a future where young people thrive by fully utilizing their creative potential. At The Seventh, we believe we are a vital part of both the present and the future. By prioritizing and advancing the creative gifts entrusted to us by God, we inspire and equip young people to explore and nurture their talents.

We focus on empowering youth between the ages of 16 and 30 through leadership development, mentorship programs, and opportunities that prepare them to excel in the creative industry and beyond.

Define

The Seventh Creative Agency exists to empower young African creatives by providing a Christ-centered platform that celebrates their God-given talents. We are committed to building a thriving community of creatives who boldly showcase their creativity to the world while fostering personal growth and spiritual purpose.

Design

To create a safe and secure creative space where young creatives can thrive, explore their potential, and lead independent, successful lives, all built on a strong Christian foundation.

Build

To showcase the creativity of young African Christian creatives to the world, build a strong Christian creative community, and grow Christian brands globally. Youth work at The Seventh focuses on empowering young people through creative skills development, faith-based empowerment, leadership growth, and community building. We provide workshops and mentorship in graphic design, content creation, event planning, and marketing while fostering leadership skills through coaching and real-life projects.Youth work at The Seventh focuses on empowering young people through creative skills development, faith-based empowerment, leadership growth, and community building.

Launch

We provide workshops and mentorship in graphic design, content creation, event planning, and marketing while fostering leadership skills through coaching and real-life projects. Our faith-driven initiatives include worship nights, conferences, and platforms like Honest Tea for open discussions. We also facilitate networking events, social media engagement, job placements, and advocacy for youth representation in creative and faith-based spaces. Guided by our core values—creativity, faith, excellence, impact, collaboration, integrity, cultural relevance, and community—we are dedicated to transforming lives, enriching communities, and celebrating Ghanaian culture while embracing global influences.

Our Expertise

At The Seventh Creative Agency, we bring creativity to the forefront of everything we do...

Ensuring that every service we offer is unique, impactful, and tailored to deliver results. Here's how we do it:

Marketing

Marketing is more than just a strategy for delivering products and services—it’s an art form. At The Seventh, we focus on the creative aspects of marketing, ensuring that every campaign is as compelling as it is effective. We believe that creativity makes a product or service not just presentable but irresistible.

Advertising

Advertising is the bridge between a great product and its audience. At The Seventh, we know that creativity is the key to impactful advertising. A well-crafted advertisement can transform a good product or service into a bestseller. That’s why we focus on creating ads that captivate and convert.

Public Relations

Public relations is the art of managing perceptions. At The Seventh, we excel at using earned media to shape how people view your brand. By focusing on building a positive reputation and maintaining trust, we ensure your brand connects meaningfully with its audience.

Event Planning and Management

We specialize in planning and managing Christian and corporate events with a deep understanding of Ghanaian culture. Creativity is at the core of our approach, ensuring that every event is unique, memorable, and impactful—all while keeping God at the center of our efforts.

Social Media Management and Marketing

In today’s digital age, social media is a powerful tool for growth. We help businesses and organizations enhance their online presence through creative content, strategic management, and innovative campaigns. While you focus on your core activities, we take charge of growing and engaging your social media audience.

Tourism

At The Seventh, we approach tourism with a Christian perspective, focusing on showcasing the beauty of God's creation and the creative works of humanity. Our mission is to create experiences that inspire awe and appreciation for the divine artistry reflected in nature and culture. We collaborate with local and international organizations to provide travelers with meaningful and enriching journeys. By partnering with tourist attractions, hotels, galleries, and event organizers, we craft experiences that highlight Ghana's unique cultural heritage while celebrating the Creator behind it all.

Brand Identity

A strong brand identity is essential for success. At The Seventh, we create distinctive brand personalities that resonate with audiences. From visual designs to messaging, we ensure that your brand stands out and attracts the right customers.

Content Production

We bring ideas to life through high-quality, creative content. From videos and graphics to written materials, our content production team ensures that your message is clear, engaging, and impactful.
